Biography

My name is Patrick Zakharchenko, since childhood I was fond of computer games and one day I discovered Fortnite. At first, I just played with friends, day by day I was addicted to the game with its amazing gameplay and that my level of play was getting higher every day. My esports career started with the Fortnite Summer Skirmish Tournament where I managed to win $34,000 for two. And very soon the Gambit Esports team became interested in me!

In the period from 2018-2020, I managed to win over $215,000, as well as play in such major tournaments as FNCS Season X, Australian Open 2020, Secret Skirmish and many others.

Now I'm a personal trainer! And I want to share my experience with people who want to start progressing faster in the game, and in the future - win tournaments!
